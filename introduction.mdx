# Welcome to Workflow Engine Platform

The Workflow Engine Platform is a comprehensive solution for building async workflow engines with human-in-the-loop processing. Design complex approval workflows, manage task assignments with priority escalation, and maintain complete audit trails—all powered by a visual workflow builder and robust execution engine.

## What is Workflow Engine Platform?

Workflow Engine Platform enables you to create reusable workflow templates where each step can trigger human approvals, automated actions, or child workflows. Built on a foundation of immutable versioning and database-backed state management, it ensures your long-running business processes remain reliable and auditable.

## Architecture Overview

The platform separates **static workflow definitions** from **runtime execution data**:

- **Static side**: Workflow definitions, steps, transitions, assignment rules, approval policies, and notification templates
- **Runtime side**: Workflow instances, step instances, human tasks, action history, notifications, and subworkflow links

This separation ensures you can edit and version workflow definitions without corrupting in-flight workflows.

## Use Cases

Workflow Engine Platform is ideal for:

- **Approval workflows**: Purchase orders, expense approvals, document reviews
- **Multi-stage processes**: Onboarding, loan applications, compliance reviews
- **Human + automation**: Combine automated steps with human decision points
- **Long-running workflows**: Workflows that pause for days or weeks waiting for approvals
